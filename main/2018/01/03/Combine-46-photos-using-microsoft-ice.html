Photos to Facebook 360

A tutorial of converting mulitple photos to spherical 360 photos for Facebook.

1. Combine 2. Rezise 3. Tag

This tutorial demonstrate the steps of:

  1. Combine multiple photos to spherical 360 panorama.

Steps:

  1. “New Panorama From Imagaes” ➜ Select your set of images ➜ Open ➜ Next

  2. Use default settings (Camera Motion: Auto-detect) ➜ Next

  3. Use default settings (Projection: Spherical) ➜ Next

  4. Click Auto completion ➜ Adjust the area you want to include in the panorama (for spherical 360 panorama, you need to make sure the side of chopped image can be connect to another side smoothly)

  5. Quality set to 100 ➜ Export to disk
